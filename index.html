What is Rat ?

Remote Administration Tool

A remote administration tool (RAT) is a software program

that gives you the ability to control another device remotely.

You then have access to the device’s system as if you had

physical access to the device itself. This remote access can be

used to turn the device on or off, troubleshoot problems,

access files, and more.

About

R3tr0 Rat

R3tr0 Rat lets you extensively control and manage one or many Android Phones remotely.It’s the perfect solution if you need to use your Mobile from a remote location, or if you need to oversee an entire network of Phones from a single spot, having full control on each one of them.Craxs has been designed to provide you with High performance, speed, and Stable ConnectionForm Android 5 up to Android 12

Compatibility

R3tr0 Rat compatible with any Mobile Running Android OS , and Support android from V5 up to Android V12.

Communication

R3tr0 Rat provides high connection speed , with stability , and security , and can work on local network or internal network.

Real Time

R3tr0 Rat Gives you real-time Monitoring and Controlling to your phones screen plus you camera,location,microphone,and more..

Data Back-up

R3tr0 Rat automatically (optional) Back-up your data such as contacts,images,sms,accounts,applications,and more.

Send & Receive

Craxs rat allows you to Upload or Download any data from your mobile such as files,videos,images,songs, and any kind if data.

Protection

craxs rat allows you to enter your connection key between pc and mobile , to prevent any access to your clients..
